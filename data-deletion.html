Account & Data Deletion — Fitfamly

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Fitfamly account and the personal data associated with it. Fitfamly is developed and operated by Venkat, and is available on Google Play.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (immediate)

You can delete your account and all associated data yourself, at any time, without contacting us:

  1. Open the Fitfamly app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap 🗑️ Delete Account.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete Everything.
Deletion is processed immediately and cannot be undone. You will be signed out automatically once it completes.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app or your account, email venkaat92@gmail.com from the email address registered with your Fitfamly account, with the subject line Delete My Account.

We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days of the request, and confirm by email once it is done.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently erased from our systems:

  • Your family members' profiles — names, ages, gender, height and weight
  • Dietary preferences, cuisine preferences, allergies, and nutrition goals
  • All generated meal plans, cook briefs, and meal history
  • Your fridge and pantry inventory
  • Daily feedback, meal ratings, and outside-food logs
  • Subscription and cheat-credit usage records
  • Your push-notification token and device identifiers
  • Your name, email address, phone number, and city
  • Your password and any linked Google, Apple, Facebook, Microsoft or LinkedIn sign-in identity

What is retained, and for how long

DataRetentionWhy
An anonymised account record (no name, no phone, no email content) Retained Marked deleted so the same account cannot be silently reactivated, and to keep an audit trail of the deletion itself
Payment and transaction records Up to 8 years Required by Indian tax and financial record-keeping law. Held by our payment processor, Razorpay — we never store card details

No retained record can be used to identify you, contact you, or rebuild your family profile.

Your rights

Fitfamly complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India). You may request access to, correction of, or erasure of your personal data at any time. You can also download a full copy of your data from the Profile screen in the app before deleting your account.
