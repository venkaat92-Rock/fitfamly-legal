Privacy Policy — Fitfamly

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Fitfamly ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered family meal-planning application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and protect it, and the choices and rights you have. By creating an account and using Fitfamly, you agree to the practices described here.

Fitfamly is designed to be operated by an adult (a parent or guardian) who manages meal planning on behalf of their household, which may include children. We take the privacy of all family members — especially children — seriously, and we comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and Google Play's Families policy.

1. Who is responsible for your data

Fitfamly is the data controller for the information you provide. For any privacy question or request, contact us at venkaat92@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address (and name, if you provide it), used to create and secure your account.
  • If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic Google profile (name, email) via secure OAuth. We never see your Google password.
  • Phone number, only if you choose phone-based sign-in.
  • Your city (optional), to tailor cuisine and grocery suggestions.

Family & health profile

To generate meal plans, the account holder enters information about each family member, which may include:

  • Name or label, age, and gender
  • Optional height and weight (used to estimate calorie needs)
  • Dietary preferences (vegetarian / non-vegetarian), cuisine preferences, and meals per day
  • Food allergies and, optionally, skin type — so the planner can avoid unsuitable foods
  • Nutrition goals (e.g. stay balanced, weight loss, build muscle)
Data about children. If a household includes children, the parent or guardian who holds the account enters and controls that data on the child's behalf. Children do not create accounts, sign in, make purchases, or use the app directly. Children's data is used only to generate appropriate family meal plans. We do not use it for advertising or profiling, and we do not knowingly allow children to provide data to us directly.

App activity

  • Meal plans generated for you, dishes you swap, and daily feedback you log
  • Your kitchen / fridge inventory, if you choose to add it
  • Subscription status and cheat-credit usage

Payment information

Subscription payments are processed by our payment partner, Razorpay. Card and banking details are entered directly with Razorpay and are not stored on our servers. We retain only your subscription status and transaction identifiers.

Technical & device data

  • Basic device and app information and crash/error diagnostics, to keep the app stable (via Sentry).
  • A push-notification token, if you enable notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging).

3. How we use your information

  • To generate and personalise AI meal plans for your family
  • To operate core features — fridge inventory, feedback, cook plans, subscriptions
  • To send you app notifications you have enabled (e.g. "your plan is ready")
  • To provide customer support and keep the app secure and reliable
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. AI processing

To build meal plans, relevant profile and preference data (such as dietary preferences, allergies, family size, and goals) is sent to our AI provider, Anthropic, which generates the plan and returns it to us. This data is used only to fulfil your request. We do not sell your data, and it is not used to train third-party models for unrelated purposes.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers who help us run the app, and only as needed:

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Where your data is stored & how we protect it

  • Your data is stored in India (Mumbai region), in line with the DPDP Act, 2023.
  • Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS.
  • Access is restricted and protected by authentication and access controls.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Access & download: You can download all of your data at any time from the Profile screen in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen, or by emailing venkaat92@gmail.com. See our Account & Data Deletion page for full step-by-step instructions.
  • Correction: You can edit family member details and preferences directly in the app.
  • Notifications: You can turn push notifications on or off from your device settings.
  • As a parent or guardian, you control all data about your children and can review or delete it at any time.

To exercise any DPDP Act right, contact venkaat92@gmail.com. We will respond within the time required by law.

8. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your personal data from our active systems, except where we must retain limited records to meet legal, tax, or security obligations.

9. Children's privacy

Fitfamly stores health and nutrition data about children only when entered by the account-holding adult, and uses it solely to provide family meal-planning. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children, do not serve them advertising, and do not build advertising profiles. If you believe a child has provided us information directly, contact venkaat92@gmail.com and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated in the app.
